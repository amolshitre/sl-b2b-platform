How to:

  1. Put these in the <head></head> section of your document: 
    <link rel="stylesheet" href="${staticContextPath}/css/jqueryui/jquery-ui-1.7.2.custom.css" type="text/css"></link>
<script type="text/javascript" src="${staticContextPath}/javascript/jquery/jquery-1.3.2.min.js"></script>
<script type="text/javascript" src="${staticContextPath}/javascript/jquery/jqueryui/jquery-ui-1.7.2.custom.min.js"></script>
    Note: Don't insert duplicates if the above files have already been referenced on the page. Also, the above code assumes that you've defined the "staticContextPath" variable in your JSP.
  2. Initialize the accordion: 
    <script type="text/javascript">
	$(function() {
		$("#accordion").accordion();
	});
</script>
  3. Create the section labels and content containers: 
    <div id="accordion">
	<h3><a href="#">Section 1</a></h3>
	<div>
		<p>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
	</div>
	<h3><a href="#">Section 2</a></h3>
	<div>
		<p>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
	</div>
	<h3><a href="#">Section 3</a></h3>
	<div>
		<p>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. 
		The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
	</div>
	<h3><a href="#">Section 4</a></h3>
	<div>
		<p>
		The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
		</p>
		<p>
		The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
	</div>
</div>
