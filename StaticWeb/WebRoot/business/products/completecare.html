Complete Care

End-to-end managed services from recruiting to rating and everything in between. Work with us to customize a solution for your service needs.

The ServiceLive Team, with a combined 60 years of management experience in the services business, provides a turn-key, customizable solution for all of your services needs.

We can handle your recruiting (or onboard your existing service providers), order routing and management, and payment and rating, all designed to meet your SLAs. We can create private-label solutions for you and your customers and recruit and train providers just to your specifications.

Our state-of-the-art call center and dispatch center utilizes the latest in telephony and service order management technology. The San Antonio Support Team also manages all support requests from providers and end-customers, including rescheduling requests and communication.

It's a hands-free way to grow your services business without the need to expand internal resources or bridge experience gaps. Plus, our seamless integration process brings you on board quickly, and our detailed reporting provides you the data you need to see and manage success.

Availability of Complete Care varies by location and service vertical. Contact our team to get started.
