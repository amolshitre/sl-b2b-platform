Features

Order Routing

The ServiceLive platform immediately routes your job to the providers you choose - manually or through auto-injection.

Increased Competition

Marketplace dynamics encourage cost savings as a result of increased competition - and ratings encourage providers to do the best job possible.

Order Management Tools

The ServiceLive platform gives you the tools to manage your service orders smarter. The Service Order Monitor provides real-time updates on every service order and highlights any buyer next steps. Our IVR system allows service providers to update their status as they arrive at a customer's location and when a job is complete. Custom reference fields allow you to create your own public or private fields for your service orders.

Fast Electronic Payment

The proprietary payment platform* reduces accounting/finance resource needs and allows quick and secure electronic payments to your service providers.

Detailed Provider Profiles

Choose the best providers with the qualifications you need. You can view detailed profiles of individual providers or search by certifications, ratings, languages spoken or distance to the job site.

Ratings & Reviews

Our Marketplace providers have an average rating of 4.6/5.0. ServiceLive conducts background checks on each and every provider you can hire through the Marketplace - the same background check required by our Fortune 500 buyers.

 

Our Provider Network is Nationwide!

The ServiceLive Marketplace is made up of tens of thousands of local, independent service providers across a wide variety of service categories - from handymen and home electronics pros to plumbers and computer experts. And more are joining every day! Just hover over your state to view the number of ServiceLive-Approved pros in your area.
