Copy & Pasting the URL

1. Click on or enter the URL "http://www.searspartsdirect.com" into your browser address bar

2. Click on the "Part Number" dropdown selection and enter in your Part Number (e.g. 95405 )

3. If more than one part number is returned in the search, click on the part that you have used and display the part details

4. On the part page, click and select the entire URL displayed in your browser’s address bar. “Copy” the selected URL by pressing “CTRL-C” or “CMD-C” on your keyboard

5. Paste the copied URL into the field provided on the parts page

6. Click on the “Save URL” button to save the Part Number, Division Number, and Source Number embedded in the URL

7. Complete the rest of the form and click the “Add” button to finish entering the part
