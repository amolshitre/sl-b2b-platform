Copy & Pasting the Part's Division & Source Numbers

1. Click on or enter the URL "http://www.searspartsdirect.com" into your browser address bar

2. Click on the "Part Number" dropdown selection and enter in your Part Number (e.g. 95405 )

3. If more than one part is returned in the search, click on the part that you have used and display the part details

4. On the part page, double-click and copy the Division Number in the URL. The division number is the second number series in the URL. The first number is the part number you used for your search

5. Paste the copied Division Number into the field provided on the parts page

6. On the part page, double-click and copy the Source Number in the URL.  The Source Number is the third number in the URL

7. Paste the copied Source Number into the field provided on the parts page

8. Complete the rest of the form and click the "Add" button to finish entering the part
