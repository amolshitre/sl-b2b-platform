Tips for Project Description

Heating & Cooling

Note: These questions may not apply to your project

  • What kind of product(s) is/are to be serviced or installed?
  • Do other items need to connect to the product(s)?
  • Do you have all of the necessary accessories or parts? (cables, hardware, etc.)
  • How would you describe a successful service visit?
  • Is your system gas or electric?
  • How/when did you first notice the issue? (noise, gradual change in performance, etc.)
  • Is there easy access to the area involved?
  • Is a ladder required?
  • Is there access to the attic?
  • How old is the unit?
  • When was your unit last serviced? Any history of problems?
