Tips for Project Description

Walls & Ceilings

Note: These questions may not apply to your project

  • What type of service do you require? (painting, wallpapering, patching, drywall, etc.)
  • Do you have all of the necessary accessories or parts? (paint, spackle, etc.)
  • How would you describe a successful service visit?
  • What are the dimensions of the wall or ceiling? (3'x5'x5' 12'x12')
  • Is a ladder required?
  • What type of finish is needed? Any special requirements?
  • If painting is involved, what is the current wall color, and what is the new color?
  • Does any furniture need to be moved or covered?
  • Is any trimwork required? (moldings, etc.)
