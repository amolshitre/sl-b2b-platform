Tips for Project Description

Handyman Services

Note: These questions may not apply to your project

  • What kind of product(s) is/are to be serviced, built or installed?
  • Do you have all of the necessary accessories or parts? (ceiling fan, light fixture, hardware, etc.)
  • How would you describe a successful service visit?
  • What are the dimensions of the room? (3'x5'x5', 12'x12')
  • Is electrical or plumbing work involved? Please describe.
  • Is a ladder required?
  • Does furniture need to be moved?
  • Is the work inside or outside the house?
  • Are any special materials required?
