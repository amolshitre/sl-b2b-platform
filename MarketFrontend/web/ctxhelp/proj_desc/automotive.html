Tips for Project Description

Automotive

Note: These questions may not apply to your project

  • What kind of product(s) is/are to be serviced or installed?
  • Do you have all of the necessary accessories or parts?
  • How would you describe a successful service visit?
  • What is the year, make and model of your vehicle?
  • If there is a service issue, please describe how you discovered the problem. (noise, gradual change in performance, etc.)
  • When does the problem occur? (speed, weather, turning, accelerating, etc.)
  • Can the vehicle be driven?
  • Is there a fluid leak? What color/type of fluid?
  • How many miles has the vehicle been driven?
