Tips for Project Description

Computer/Network Services

Note: These questions may not apply to your project

  • What kind of product(s) is/are to be serviced or installed?
  • What are the other items that need to be connected to the product(s)?
  • Do you have all of the necessary accessories or parts? (cables, etc.)
  • How would you describe a successful service visit?
  • Is your computer a PC or Mac?
  • When did you notice something was wrong?
  • Who is your internet provider?
  • Do you have a wireless or wired connection?
  • Are all of the items in the same room?
  • Why are you upgrading or replacing?
  • Have you backed up your data?
  • Are there passwords required to access the system for repair?
