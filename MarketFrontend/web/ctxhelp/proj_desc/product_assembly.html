Tips for Project Description

Product Assembly

Note: These questions may not apply to your project

  • What kind of product(s) is/are to be assembled? (manufacturer, model #, etc.)
  • Do you have all of the necessary accessories or parts? (hardware, manuals, etc.)
  • How would you describe a successful service visit?
  • What room/floor will the product be assembled on?
  • Is the area where the product will be placed/installed clear of furniture?
