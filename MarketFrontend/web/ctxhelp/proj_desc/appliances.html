Tips for Project Description

Appliances

Note: These questions may not apply to your project

  • What kind of product(s) is to be serviced or installed?
  • Do other items need to be connected to the product?
  • Do you have all of the necessary accessories or parts? (cables, hardware, etc.)
  • How would you describe a successful service visit?
  • What room/floor will the work be on?
  • Is there a leak involved? Can you identify the source?
  • If an appliance is malfunctioning, can you describe how you discovered the problem? (noise, gradual change, etc.)
  • Is there a plumbing, gas or electrical connection involved?
  • Will you need to dispose of any replaced appliances?
  • Is the appliance gas or electric?
