Tips for Project Description

Home Electronics

Note: These questions may not apply to your project

  • What kind of product(s) is/are to be serviced or installed?
  • What are the other items that need to be connected to the product(s)?
  • Do you have all of the necessary accessories or parts needed? (brackets, cables, etc.)
  • How would you describe a successful service visit?
  • Will the product be mounted to a wall? What type? (drywall, brick, etc.)
  • What is the make, model, size and brand of your product?
  • Who is your cable TV or satellite provider?
  • Are any remotes being consolidated?
  • How big is the room? What room?
  • Do you have an electrical outlet near?
  • Will any cables or wiring need to be buried?
