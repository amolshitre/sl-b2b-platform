Tips for Project Description

Masonry & Stone

Note: These questions may not apply to your project

  • What kind of product(s) is/are to be installed?
  • Do you have all of the necessary materials?(stones, mortar, sand, etc)
  • How would you describe a successful service visit?
  • For patios and walkways, please provide area dimensions.
  • Are you replacing existing masonry/stone?
  • How will you dispose of any replaced items?
