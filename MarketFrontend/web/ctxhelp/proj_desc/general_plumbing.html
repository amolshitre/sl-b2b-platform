Tips for Project Description

General Plumbing & Bathrooms

Note: These questions may not apply to your project

  • What kind of product(s) is/are to be serviced or installed? (manufacturer, model #)
  • What are the other items connect to the product(s)?
  • Do you have all of the necessary accessories or parts?
  • How would you describe a successful service visit?
  • If a leak is involved, can you identify the source?
  • Are you replacing an existing item?
  • If there is an appliance involved, is it gas or electric?
  • How will you dispose of any replaced items?
  • Is there easy access to the area to be serviced?
  • When/how did you first notice any problems?
